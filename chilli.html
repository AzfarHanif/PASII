Chilli Crab

Chilli crab is a sweet, savory, and slightly spicy crab dish, typically made with mud crabs stir-fried in a rich tomato and chilli-based sauce. Despite its name, the dish isn't usually very spicyâ€”it's more of a balanced, flavorful sauce with a touch of heat.

Main Ingredients

> Fresh crab (usually mud crab)
> Garlic & ginger
> Chilli sauce or paste (like sambal)
> Tomato ketchup or puree
> Eggs (beaten and stirred in to give a silky texture)
> Soy sauce, sugar, vinegar
> Cornstarch slurry (to thicken the sauce)
